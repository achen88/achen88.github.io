3D Earth Animation through Simulation and Ray Tracing

Link to Github repository

I made a 3D animation of the moon and earth rotating for my CS102 (C++) final project using the Phong shading model. I made everything besides the textures, meaning I worked out physical modelling, collision detection, ray tracing, and texture mapping. To compile the mp4 from raw jpeg files, I used ImageMagick.
